1

Boil Water

Bring a large pot of salted water to a rolling boil. Use 1 tablespoon of salt per liter.

2

Cook Pasta

Add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until al dente.

3

Heat Sauce

While pasta cooks, heat the tomato sauce in a separate pan over medium heat for 3-4 minutes.

4

Combine

Drain pasta, reserving 1 cup of pasta water. Toss pasta with sauce, adding pasta water as needed.

5

Serve

Plate immediately, garnish with fresh basil and freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano.
